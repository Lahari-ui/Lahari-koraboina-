EduPath

One-Stop Personalized Career & Education Advisor for Students in J&K

🚨 The Problem

Students in Jammu & Kashmir often struggle with choosing the right career or course due to lack of guidance, awareness, and digital access — especially in rural areas.

💡 Our Solution

EduPath is a personalized platform that suggests career options, relevant government college courses, and scholarships — all tailored to the student's interests, skills, and location. It works across smartphones, basic phones (SMS/IVR), and Common Service Centers.

🌟 Key Features

🎯 Personalized Career Advice

Students receive smart career recommendations based on their inputs — like interests, subjects, and skills.

🏫 Govt College Courses

Find courses available in local government colleges aligned with their chosen career path.

💰 Scholarships & Schemes

View government scholarships students are eligible for, based on their profile and income category.

📞 IVR & SMS Support

Students in rural areas can access career suggestions via toll-free numbers and SMS without internet.

🏢 CSC Integration

Common Service Centers (CSCs) help students in offline areas access EduPath with operator assistance.

🌐 Multilingual Support

Supports English, Hindi, and Urdu to ensure inclusivity across the region.

Empowering every student in J&K

Whether online or offline, EduPath ensures no student is left behind in choosing the right career and course.
